Frequently Asked Questions

Is the M4 Max overkill for standard web development?

Yes. If your team purely writes React applications and relies on cloud staging, the standard M3 or M4 will suffice. The M4 Max is specifically for local virtualization, AI model running, and heavy data science operations.

Can I upgrade the RAM or Storage later?

No. Apple Silicon architecture uses Unified Memory and soldered SSDs. You must configure your required capacity at the point of purchase, which makes forecasting your 3-year enterprise needs highly critical.

How many external monitors can the M4 Max support?

The M4 Max chip natively supports up to four external displays (three at 6K and one at 4K), making it the ultimate hub for a multi-monitor trading desk or massive debugging arrays.

Is the battery life sufficient for long flights?

Absolutely. Even with the powerful Max chip, macOS optimization ensures 14-18 hours of typical use, allowing unbroken productivity on long-haul business trips.
