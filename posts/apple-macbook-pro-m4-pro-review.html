Expert Analysis: FAQ

Is the M4 Pro overkill for standard web development?

For most web development, the M4 Pro is actually the 'sweet spot' for performance. It handles large Docker environments and local staging effortlessly without the massive price jump of the Max chip.

How many external monitors can the M4 Pro support?

The M4 Pro chip natively supports up to two external displays with high resolution and refresh rates, compared to four on the Max.

Is the battery life sufficient for long flights?

Absolutely. The M4 Pro is extremely efficient, often providing 18-22 hours of battery life on a single charge.
