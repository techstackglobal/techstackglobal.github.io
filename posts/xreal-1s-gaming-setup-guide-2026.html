How to Use XREAL 1S with Steam Deck

The Steam Deck OLED remains the ultimate companion for the XREAL 1S. To get started, follow these deployment steps:

  1. Connect Glass: Plug the XREAL 1S USB-C cable directly into the top port of the Steam Deck.
  2. Handheld UI: The Deck will automatically detect the glasses as a secondary monitor. The built-in screen will turn off.
  3. Refresh Rate Unlock: Navigate to Settings > Display and manually set the resolution to 1920x1080 and the refresh rate to 120Hz.
  4. OSD Control: Use the side-buttons on the XREAL 1S to adjust brightness and volume as needed.

Pro Strategy: Because the Steam Deck only has a single USB-C port, we strongly recommend the XREAL Hub. This allows you to charge your Deck at full-power speeds while maintaining the 120-Hz video link. How to Use XREAL 1S with Nintendo Switch

To use AR glasses with the Nintendo Switch, you must satisfy the console's "Docked Mode" requirements. You cannot simply plug the glasses into the Switch's bottom port.

  1. Prepare the Hub: Connect your Nintendo Switch to the host-input on an XREAL Hub.
  2. Apply Power: Plug a 45W+ PD power bank into the charging port of the hub.
  3. Link Glasses: Connect the XREAL 1S to the designated output port on the hub.
  4. Verification: The Switch screen should go black, and the 120-inch virtual display will appear in your glasses.

This setup allows for a "Zero-G" gaming experience where you can hold a Joy-Con in each hand while lying flat on your back, removing all strain from your neck and wrists.

How to Use XREAL 1S with PS5 & Xbox Series X

For home consoles, the setup is slightly more static but provides the ultimate "Private Studio" vibe. This is ideal for late-night gaming without waking up the household.

  • HDMI Connection: Connect an HDMI-to-USB-C adapter to the HDMI output of your PS5/Xbox.
  • USB Power: Connect the adapter's power lead to a USB port on the front of your console.
  • Final Link: Plug the XREAL 1S directly into the adapter.

Important Note: Most HDMI adapters are currently limited to 60Hz. If you want the full 120Hz console experience, you must use a specialized high-bandwidth adapter capable of processing DP 1.4 signals.

How to Use XREAL 1S with ROG Ally & Ally X

The ASUS ROG Ally X is currently the best handheld for AR gaming because it includes two separate USB-C ports. This removes the need for expensive hubs entirely.

Simply plug your charging cable into one port and the XREAL 1S into the other. This setup supports the full 120Hz refresh rate and FreeSync, making titles like Modern Warfare III feel buttery smooth on a 120-inch virtual screen.

Common XREAL 1S Gaming Setup Problems

  • No Video Signal: ensure your host device supports DP Alt Mode. Most desktop PC motherboards do NOT support this via their USB-C ports unless they have a dedicated Thunderbolt link.
  • Charging Issues: If using a hub, ensure your power brick provides at least 45W of output. Low-wattage phone chargers will not trigger "Docked Mode" on the Nintendo Switch.
  • Screen Drift: If the screen slowly moves to the side, recalibrate the IMU via the Nebula app or the Beam Pro settings. Resetting your position usually takes less than 2 seconds.
  • Lag Problems: Avoid using long, third-party USB-C extension cables. The XREAL 1S requires high-bandwidth data; cheap cables can introduce input latency or visual artifacts.

The Hidden Variable: Audio Spatiality

The built-in speakers in the XREAL 1S are excellent for mid-height cues, but for total immersion in Elden Ring or Starfield, you need the Audio Blueprint. The XREAL 1S supports directional audio, but we recommend pairing them with the Sony WF-1000XM5 or AirPods Pro 2 via your host device. By offloading the audio to high-fidelity earbuds, you free up the glasses to focus purely on the visual data stream.

Gaming Setup FAQ

Can I use the XREAL 1S with PS5?

Yes. You will need an HDMI to USB C adapter and a power source. We recommend the official XREAL HDMI adapter for the best compatibility and 120Hz support.

How much battery does it pull from my Switch?

On average, the XREAL 1S adds a 15-20% drain on your device's battery since it has no internal power source. For flights over 3 hours, a PD-rated power bank is highly recommended.

Does the XREAL 1S support HDR for gaming?

Yes, but it depends on the host device. The Steam Deck OLED and ROG Ally support HDR output over USB-C, which the XREAL 1S can maintain with peak brightness levels over 600 nits.
