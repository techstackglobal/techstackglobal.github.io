The Speed Comparison Explained

To put this in perspective, think of the connection between your storage and your processor as a highway.

  • SATA SSDs (The Old Standard): Like a two-lane road. Speeds max out around 500 MB/s.
  • PCIe Gen3 NVMe: Like a four-lane highway. Speeds hit around 3,500 MB/s. It feels incredibly fast.
  • PCIe Gen4 NVMe (Samsung 990 PRO): Like an eight-lane autobahn with no speed limit. It hits a staggering 7,450 MB/s.

That means the 990 PRO can move a massive 50GB file in less than 10 seconds under ideal conditions.

The hard truth is that if you use your computer strictly for browsing the web, answering emails, and writing documents, you will not notice the difference between a standard Gen3 NVMe and the 990 PRO. Both will boot your PC in seconds and load word processors instantly.
