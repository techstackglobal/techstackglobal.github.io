Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with iPhone 15/16/17?

Yes, any iPhone with a USB-C port works natively for screen mirroring. For full spatial apps, the XREAL Beam Pro is recommended.

Is it better than the Meta Quest 3?

For productivity and media consumption, yes. It's much lighter and clearer. For VR gaming (Beat Saber, etc.), the Quest is still superior.

Can I use this as my main work monitor?

Absolutely. Many remote workers use the Nebula software (available for Mac and Windows) to create three virtual screens. The text clarity on the 1S is sharp enough for coding and document editing.

Do I need to charge the glasses?

No. The XREAL 1S is powered by the device it's plugged into (phone, laptop, console) via the USB-C cable. It has no internal battery, which keeps it incredibly lightweight.
